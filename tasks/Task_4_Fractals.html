Task 4: Generate a fractal geographic distribution of a species

Petr Keil

January 22, 2018

1 Objective

Your objective is to write a simple function or program or script that will generate fractal distribution of a species on a grid. Ideally a raster object.

1.1 Examples of fractal distributions:

Keil, Wilson & Jetz (2014) Diversity and Distributions

Halley et al. (2004) Ecology Letters

Storch et al. (2008) Ecology Letters

1.3 Papers on fractals in ecology

  • Brown et al. 2002 on fractal nature of nature.
  • Lennon et al. 2002 makes the point that fractal species distributions do not produce power-law species area relationship.
  • Halley et al. 2004 provide, to my knowledge, the most useful and authoritative practical intro to fractals in ecology.
  • Storch et al. 2008 idea of “generalized fractals” in ecology.
